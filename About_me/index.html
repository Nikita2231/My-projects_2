about me

who am i

The adjective that characterizes me better than others is creative. I have been writing poetry since 2015. I also participate in poetry evenings & competitions, where I can read my pieces from the stage. My other hobby is drawing with a simple pencil. Portraits turn out to be "alive" and make people wonder at their complexity and technicality. Last but not least, I devote my free evenings to playing the guitar.

At school, I was attracted by exact sciences the most, such as mathematics, physics, and chemistry. I enjoyed performing long calculations and solving complex problems.

After school, I decided to connect my professional life with programming and entered the MPEI. At the University, I studied Pascal, C ++, C #, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

To me, coding is one of the most engaging areas of activity that allows me to create something of my own by combining creative and mathematical approaches.
